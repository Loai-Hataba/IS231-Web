About Conquista Beblio

Bridging Egypt’s literary past with its digital future

Our Heritage

Since 1997, Conquista Beblio has grown from a small archive in Cairo to a nationally recognized online library. We started with a focus on Egyptian literature and history and have expanded to include over 200,000 Arabic and English volumes, covering a wide range of topics and time periods.

We are committed to preserving Egypt’s literary legacy while making it available to a new generation of readers. Our unique approach blends traditional conservation methods with cutting-edge digital tools, allowing readers worldwide to explore our collection.

A library is not just a place for books—it’s a gateway to ideas, stories, and identities. At Conquista Beblio, we carry forward the spirit of ancient Egyptian knowledge into the digital age.
– Dr. Amira El-Sayed, Founder

Our Collections

We house a diverse selection of materials including ancient scrolls, modern novels, research journals, and children’s books. Our bilingual library supports both Arabic and English readers, with a growing number of translated works and educational content.

What We Offer

Conquista Beblio delivers a seamless blend of in-person and online library experiences. Whether you're in Cairo or browsing from abroad, our library provides access to Egypt’s rich literary heritage and a wide range of contemporary works in both Arabic and English.

  • Rare manuscripts and bilingual publications available to all users
  • Global digital access to our growing online library
  • Expert librarians in Egyptology, Arabic, and comparative literature
  • Live cultural sessions, author talks, and workshops
  • Translation support for ancient and classical texts
  • Research support for academics, educators, and students
