About Conquista Beblio

A cultural bridge between ancient knowledge and modern readers

Our Heritage

Founded in 1997,Conquista Beblio began as a small collection of Egyptian literature and historical texts in Cairo. Today, we've grown into one of Egypt's most prestigious libraries, housing over 200,000 volumes spanning 5,000 years of Egyptian and world history.

Our mission is to preserve Egypt's literary heritage while making knowledge accessible to everyone. We combine traditional preservation techniques with modern digital archiving to ensure these treasures are available for generations to come.

What We Offer

AtConquista Beblio, we provide a blend of traditional library services and modern digital access. Our facilities in Cairo include reading rooms, research spaces, and cultural event venues.

  • Access to rare Egyptian manuscripts and contemporary publications
  • Digital archives accessible worldwide through our online platform
  • Expert librarians specializing in Egyptology and Arabic literature
  • Regular cultural events, readings, and educational workshops
  • Translation services for ancient Egyptian texts
  • Research assistance for scholars and students
